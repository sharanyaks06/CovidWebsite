how to prevent virus

wash your place

Regular household cleaning and disinfection products will effectively eliminate the virus from the surfaces.For cleaning and disinfecting households with suspected or confirmed COVID-19 surface virucidal disinfectent,such as 0.05% sodium hypochlorite and products based on ethanol should be used

maintain social distance

In public health, social distancing, also called physical distancing, is a set of non-pharmaceutical interventions or measures intended to prevent the spread of a contagious disease by maintaining a physical distance between people and reducing the number of times people come into close contact with each other

dont touch face

To reduce the spread of the novel coronavirus (COVID-19), experts recommend that you not touch your face because the virus is transmitted through mucous membranes in the mouth, nose and eyes.

wash your hands

Washing hands can keep you healthy and prevent the spread of respiratory and diarrheal infections from one person to the next.

use napkins

Napkins are clean substances. These help to wipe your face and hands in case you have touched your face or touched anything outdoor. Always have a pack of napkins.

wear a mask

A very important measure is using face mask. It acts as shield between you and the people. It helps you from outer infections. Also it helps others if you are affected or you are tested positive.
